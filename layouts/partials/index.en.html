Venue

SnowCamp will take place in "La Maison Minatec" - Conference Center

Both Universities and Conferences will take place at "La Maison Minatec", a Conference Center in the heart of Grenoble.


3 Parvis Louis Néel
38000 Grenoble


You can access:

  • by Tramway line B (towards Grenoble Presqu'île), stop "Cité Internationale"
  • by Car (you can park at the parking Doyen Weil or Europole Gare)
    • when arriving from Lyon or Valence, take Exit 16 off of the N481; go over the Oxford bridge and then follow signs to MINATEC via "Rue des Martyrs"
    • when arriving from Chambéry, Gap or Sisteron, take Exit 2 off of the A480; follow signs to MINATEC via "Rue Félix Esclangon"
  • by bike
